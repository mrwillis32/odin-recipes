Worlds's Best Lasagna

This is the world's best lasagna truly made with love.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatos
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
